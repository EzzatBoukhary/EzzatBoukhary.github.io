The Idea

A fully interactive hardware-connected virtual pet inspired by Tamagotchi. You hatch your dragon, care for it, and watch it physically react through a live LED display while controlling everything from your phone.

How It Works

  • Built native mobile app using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose
  • Real-time sync between hardware and mobile powered by Firebase
  • ESP8266 microcontroller driving a 16x16 LED grid for live pixel animations
  • Persistent state even when offline for uninterrupted gameplay

Achievements

  • Delivered fully functional interactive pet experience in just 36 hours
  • Engineered robust real-time state management across hardware and app
  • Designed full interaction system including feeding, cleaning, play and death mechanics
  • Handled mid-hackathon backend migration from WebSockets to Firebase for better stability
  • Created original artwork, sprites, and animations entirely in-house
  • Judges highlighted both polish and creativity leading to Best Design Award

Why This Was Fun

Having your code literally breathe life into a real physical object is hard to describe. Watching my dragon react live on hardware while controlling it from my app was easily one of the most satisfying moments I've had as a developer.

Collaboration

Built as a team of three. I led development for gameplay logic, item interaction system, mobile app interface, state syncing, and actively collaborated on hardware integration.
